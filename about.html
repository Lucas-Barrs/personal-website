Crochet

I have been crocheting for many years. My granny is a life long artist, and began teaching me at the age of 6. In the beginning, I never started or finished my own projects but my granny was there to help me every step of the way. My relationship with her has always been very special. She has always been my biggest supporter, even when I didn't believe in myself. Through middle and high school, I was inconsistent with how often I practiced. When I moved away to Philly as an adult, I missed my family and began crocheting more consitently to feel closer. It's been three years since then and having this hobby brings me so much joy both for the time to relax and create and the projects that come out of it. I have created many baby blankets stitched with love for family and friends. I also made a (very oversized) cardigan for my best friend's birthday after he moved to Colorado. I hope to use this joy for creating to help inspire future projects.
